Specimen Printing Services

01

Material Science Specimens

Tensile bars, hardness test blocks, and fracture specimens in any engineering material. ASTM and ISO standard geometry. Consistent batch production for statistically valid material comparison studies.

02

Educational Element Sets

Periodic table element demonstration specimens — pure metal samples in standardized cube or disc form. Iron, copper, titanium, aluminum, and rare earth metals. Safe, durable, and size-consistent for classroom and museum use.

03

Research Sample Production

Custom geometry research samples in specified materials for academic and industrial research. Material traceability certificates, dimensional inspection reports, and chemical composition verification supplied.
